APPLICATIONS

Gatineau Activities

Check the list of recreational and sports activities that are offered in the city of Gatineau. The list of activities is downloaded from the new open data portal of the city Gatineau (http://www.gatineau.ca/donneesouvertes/) and stored in the application for quick access without requiring a connection to the wireless data network.
